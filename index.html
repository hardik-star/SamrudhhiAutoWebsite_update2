About Company

Samruddhi Autoworld Pvt Ltd was incorporated in September, 2013 with a view to distribute spare parts for BOSCH-Automotive Aftermarket division. Later with great customer service the Company was able to receive distributorships from many other Automotive Spare parts manufacturing Company.

Viral Shah, 28, Director of the Company always had a vision to expand the network to other Countries. In 2014 the Company in partnership with others invested in the Uganda - The east African country and entered into whole new segment of Energy Conservation Solutions which was the need of the hour. The Company focused on products for Solving the energy related issues for industries and with a mission of Protecting the Mother earth from depleting resources and environment.

With Successful expansion in Uganda, Tanzania, UAE and South Africa the Company has now decided to focus on energy conservation in India and making India a pollution free country. With this Vision the Company has tied up with PowerO2 (device to reduce fuel consumption and emissions) manufacturers and has received exclusive distribution rights for the Country.

The Company is very much optimistic about taking this step into reversing the Global warming and protecting the Nature.
