About Company

We are energy experts bringing unique technologies for reduction in consumption of fuels like Diesel, Petrol LPG and CNG especially for All Four Wheelers and DG Sets.

We also deal with Eco-Friendly Fuels which can be used in all Diesel Engines, Diesel Generators, Boilers, Hot Water Generators, Air Pre-Heaters, Furnaces, Earth Moving, Mining and Material Handling Equipment creating clean environment.

OUR BUSINESS

Power O2 – A patented device which can be installed on all Diesel & Petrol Engines and equivalent applications for saving significant amount of fuel and money

Blended Bio Diesel - Suitable for All Diesel Engines and equivalent applications for saving significant fuel cost and improve performance
