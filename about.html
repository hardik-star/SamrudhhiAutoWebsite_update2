INTRODUCTION

Samruddhi Autoworld Pvt Ltd was incorporated in September, 2013 with a view to supply quality spare parts for Automobiles. The business was started with distribution of spare parts for BOSCH-Automotive Aftermarket division.

Exceptional customer service and positive reputation of the Company gained customer loyalty very quickly and was able to enter in to channel partnership with many automotive spare parts manufacturing companies.

Viral Shah; Director of the Company always had a vision to expand the network to other Countries. In Financial Year 2014; the Company invested capital in Uganda - an east African country and entered into a sustainable segment of energy conservation. The focus is purely on products and customized solutions to optimize and reduce consumption of Fuel, Oil, Water and Electricity.

With successful expansion in Uganda, Tanzania, UAE and South Africa in past few years; the Company decided to concentrate on innovative ideas which can help to reduce the fuel consumption by all types of Internal Combustion Engines (ICE).

With this thought process; the Company has tied up with well-known OEMs for POWER O2 and Blended Bio Diesel and has the exclusive distribution rights for Sales and Service of both products on Pan India basis.

OUR VISION

To become the best energy saving company through specialised energy solutions and positive attitude
