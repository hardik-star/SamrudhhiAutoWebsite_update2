Power O2

  1. Product
  2. Power O2

Introduction

  1. Power O2 is ‘PATENTED’ Clean Energy Technology with a target to substantially save all types of fuel in Internal Combustion Engines (ICE).

  2. It is a ‘COMPLETE COMBUSTION TECHNIOLOGY’ invented by Automobile Design Experts after an extensive research of almost ten years.

  3. Power O2 is an external attachment which provides enriched and homogenous mixture of oxygen to Engine for ideal air- fuel mixture. Hot air enriched with Oxygen ensures high burning efficiency and maximum heat output from engine.

  4. The installation is easy and takes hardly thirty minutes with NO alterations to engine.

Function

  1. Power O2 is clamped to exhaust gas manifold and air is sucked which then passes through a cavity having intermetallic compound.

  2. Intermetallic compound ensures optimum heat conductivity and catalytic action to enrich the air with O2

  3. The adapter produces homogeneous mixture required for atomization which burns completely

  4. Almost 97.5% of fuel is atomized in ﬁne vapor form and burns completely improving eﬃciency, reducing fuel consumption, avoiding wastage and pollution
