Introduction

  1. Blended Bio-diesel is a renewable fuel manufactured from methanol and vegetable oil, animal fats, and recycled cooking fats.

  2. It is compatible with existing diesel engines and can be used alone or blended in specific proportions. Biodiesel blends can also be used as heating oil and is a perfect substitute for HSD.

  3. Blended bio diesel can be safely used in All Types of Diesel Engines, DG Sets, Boilers, Earth moving machines, Mining Equipment and Material Handling machines & Equivalent applications


BENEFITS

  1. Does not require modifications to a SYSTEM in which it is to be used

  2. Safe to handle and Store

  3. Clean burning better ignition

  4. Higher heat output reducing fuel consumption

  5. Lower sulphur content

  6. Reduced exhaust emissions & lower toxicity

  7. Saves significant money towards fuel cost


APPLICATIONS

  1. All Diesel Engines / Vehicles

  2. Boilers

  3. Hot water Generators

  4. DG Sets and similar Emergency Services

  5. Construction Equipment

  6. Mining and Earth moving machines

  7. Boats & Ships

  4. Cleaner emissions
