Introduction
What Is Bio Diesel ?

Technically speaking, Biodiesel is a renewable fuel manufactured from methanol and vegetable oil, animal fats, and recycled cooking fats.

Biodiesel only refers to 100% pure fuel that meets the definition above

What is ‘Blended Diesel’ ?

Blended diesel is a mixture of bio diesel + ‘petroleum diesel fuel’ (or called Mineral Diesel) in specific proportion intended to be used as a substitute for HSD

Blended bio diesel can be safely used in Boilers, DG sets, any Diesel engine and Earth moving machines

Benefits of Blended Diesel
  1. Cheaper in Price as compared to HSD

  2. It is a clean burning fuel with better ignition and Combustion .

  3. Has lower moisture content and give higher heat output reducing fuel consumption

  4. Does not require modifications to a Diesel engine / DG / Boiler in which it is to be used

  5. HIt has reduced exhaust emissions and lower toxicity compared to HSD

  6. Safe to handle


Environment & Safety
  1. Blended Diesel has substantial reduction in unburned hydrocarbons, carbon monoxide and particulate matter in exhaust fumes.

  2. Sulphur dioxide emissions are significantly reduced resulting in non - offensive exhaust and doesn't cause eye irritation.

  3. Blended Diesel burns cleaner than normal HSD

  4. Diesel Engines / Boilers do not require any modifications

  5. Blended Diesel is environment friendly and more biodegradable

  6. Studies show that Bio diesel and it’s blends dramatically reduce potential risks of cancer and birth defects and help preserve natural resources.
